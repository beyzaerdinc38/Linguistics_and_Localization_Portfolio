## 📂 Project Summaries

### 🎬 1. Media & Audio-Visual Localization
*   **Madagascar (2005) Translation Analysis:** A comprehensive study analyzing how cultural, humorous, and brand-specific elements (e.g., Ziploc, Kwanzaa) were adapted into Turkish culture[cite: 4].
*   **The Fellowship of the Ring Style Review:** An analysis focused on translating J.R.R. Tolkien's archaic and complex narrative style into natural Turkish[cite: 2].
*   **Dalin Jingle Transcreation:** A creative translation (transcreation) project on adapting an iconic Turkish children's commercial rhyme into English while maintaining rhythm and tone[cite: 1].

### 🤖 2. Corpus Linguistics & Computational Semantics
*   **A Corpus-Based Study on Lexical Behaviors (Node Word: "anahtar"):** An empirical research paper utilizing the Turkish National Corpus (TNC) with over 50 million words[cite: 5]. Investigated the semantic preferences, collocations, colligations, and semantic prosody of the polysemous word "anahtar" using Sinclair’s Seven-Stage Procedure[cite: 5]. Proved how structural patterns directly determine word sense disambiguation in contemporary Turkish[cite: 5].
